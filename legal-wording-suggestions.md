## A. Terms & Conditions additions

### 1. Payment does not confirm a booking
> Paying for a quote does not create a confirmed booking. Your hotel booking is only
> confirmed once HotelRateCheck.com has completed the booking with the supplier and
> received the supplier's confirmation. We will send you a confirmation or voucher when
> this is done. Please do not make non-refundable travel arrangements (such as flights)
> until you have received that confirmation from us.

### 2. Supplier booking failure after payment
> Hotel rates and availability are live and can change at any time before a booking is
> completed. If you have paid but we are unable to complete your booking with the
> supplier — for example because the rate or availability changed, or the supplier
> declined the booking — we will either offer you a suitable alternative or refund you in
> full. You will not be charged for a booking we were unable to make. Refund timing
> depends on your bank or payment provider.

### 3. Supplier tariff / pricing error
> Quotes are based on rates provided to us by suppliers. In the rare event of an obvious
> pricing or tariff error (for example a clearly incorrect rate), we may decline or cancel
> the affected quote or booking and refund any payment taken. We will tell you promptly if
> this happens.

### 4. Local and pay-at-hotel charges
> Some hotels charge local taxes, tourism fees, resort fees or deposits that are payable by
> you directly to the hotel at check-in or check-out, and are not included in the price you
> pay HotelRateCheck.com. Where we are aware of such charges, we will show them to you
> before payment. These charges are your responsibility.

### 5. Non-refundable and non-amendable rates
> Many hotel rates are non-refundable and non-amendable. Where a rate is non-refundable or
> non-amendable, this will be shown to you before payment and you must accept it to
> proceed. Once confirmed, such bookings cannot be cancelled, changed, transferred or
> refunded except where required by law, or where the booked accommodation is not provided
> as confirmed.

### 6. Special requests not guaranteed
> Any special requests (for example bed type, room location, early check-in, adjoining
> rooms) are passed to the hotel where possible but are not guaranteed and may incur extra
> charges payable to the hotel.

### 7. No flights, packages or linked travel arrangements
> HotelRateCheck.com provides hotel accommodation only. We do not sell flights, transfers,
> packages or other travel services, and we do not sell linked travel arrangements or
> package holidays.

### 8. Check your details before paying
> Before paying you must check the hotel name, destination, dates, guest names, room type,
> board basis, total price, cancellation policy and any supplier conditions shown in your
> quote. We rely on the details you confirm.

### 9. Chargebacks and fraud
> If a payment is reversed or charged back after we have completed or committed to a
> booking on your behalf, you remain responsible for the cost of that booking. We may share
> information with our payment provider and suppliers to investigate suspected fraud.

### 10. Complaints and escalation
> If something goes wrong with your booking, contact us at quotes@hotelratecheck.com with
> your booking reference and we will help. For issues during your stay, contact the hotel
> directly in the first instance and let us know as soon as possible.

## B. Privacy Policy additions

### 1. Sharing booking data with suppliers (including outside the UK/EEA)
> To make a booking on your behalf we share the booking details you provide (such as guest
> names, contact details, dates and room details) with the accommodation supplier and the
> hotel. Some suppliers and hotels are located outside the UK and the European Economic
> Area (EEA). Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we take steps intended to
> ensure an appropriate level of protection in line with applicable data protection law.

### 2. Payment data
> Card payments are processed by our payment provider on their secure, hosted payment
> pages. We do not see or store your full card details. Your card data is handled by the
> payment provider in accordance with their terms and PCI DSS requirements.

### 3. What we collect and why
> We collect the information you give us to provide quotes and complete bookings (contact
> details and booking details), and basic technical/usage data to run and secure the site.
> We use it to respond to enquiries, make and manage bookings, handle payments and
> refunds, prevent fraud, and meet our legal obligations.

### 4. Retention
> We keep booking and payment records for as long as needed to provide the service and to
> meet legal, accounting and dispute-handling obligations, then delete or anonymise them.

### 5. Your rights
> You have rights over your personal data, including access, correction, deletion and
> objection. To exercise them, contact quotes@hotelratecheck.com.
